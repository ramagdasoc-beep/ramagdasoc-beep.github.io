About Me

I am a Senior High School ICT teacher under DepEd, specializing in TVL – Computer Systems Servicing. I graduated with a degree in Computer Science and am currently pursuing a Master of Information Systems at UPOU. Teaching is not just my profession but my passion. I enjoy guiding students through technical concepts with clear, step by step lessons and creative activities.

I’m known for being cheerful and approachable, always finding ways to make my students’ days lighter and more meaningful. My students often describe me as patient yet playful, because I believe learning should be both challenging and enjoyable. I value humility and resilience, as I openly share that I’m still learning too, making my classroom a space where growth is celebrated.

Outside of teaching, I enjoy exploring new ideas and creative approaches that make learning more engaging. Whether it’s designing activities, experimenting with fresh strategies, or finding connections between everyday life and classroom lessons, I strive to make education meaningful and accessible, even in settings where classrooms may not always be conducive to learning.

As a mother and wife, I balance professional growth with family life, finding joy in both roles. My personality is warm, empathetic, and optimistic. I approach challenges with curiosity and humor, always looking for opportunities to improve and inspire others.

I believe learning should be both challenging and enjoyable.
